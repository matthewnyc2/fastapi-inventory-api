# Products & Inventory Dashboard Screen

## Overall Vibe
Inventory management dashboard showing product catalog status and stock levels. Professional, data-driven interface with visual stock status indicators.

## Design System
- KPI metric cards showing counts
- Data table with product information
- Status badges (In Stock: green, Low Stock: orange, Out of Stock: red)
- Clean typography and spacing
- Dark theme for professional appearance

## Page Structure
Header: "Products & Inventory" with description
KPI Cards (4 columns):
- Total Products: 156
- Low Stock Items: 12
- Out of Stock: 3
- Total Categories: 8

Product Table with columns:
- Product Name
- SKU (code)
- Category
- Price
- Stock Level (numeric)
- Status Badge

Sample data showing mix of products with different stock statuses and pricing
